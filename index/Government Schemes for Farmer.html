1} PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi Details


Objective: Supplement financial needs for inputs, protect from moneylenders, boost farm income. Benefit: ₹6,000 annually in 3 equal payments (₹2,000 each). Frequency: Every four months (April-July, Aug-Nov, Dec-Mar). Transfer: Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to Aadhar-linked bank accounts. Eligibility: Landholding farmer families, with exclusions for higher-income groups/officials.

How to Access/Check

Farmers can register and check status via the official PM-KISAN portal (pmkisan.gov.in) or apps like UMANG, using Aadhaar/mobile for e-KYC.






3} pm-kusum scheme

Financial Relief: Provide subsidies for solar pumps (60% subsidy, 30% loan) to replace diesel pumps, saving costs.
Income Generation: Allow farmers to earn from selling surplus solar power to DISCOMs (Distribution Companies).
Energy Security: Promote solar energy in agriculture, supporting sustainable farming.

Three Main Components:


Component A: Setting up small (up to 2 MW) grid-connected solar power plants.
Component B: Installation of standalone solar pumps for irrigation.
Component C: Solarization (converting to solar) of existing grid-connected agricultural pumps.

Benefits for Farmers:


Reduced Energy Costs: Replaces costly diesel with cheaper solar power.
Stable Income: Earn up to ₹25,000-₹65,000 per acre annually by selling power or leasing land for solar plants.
Empowerment: Farmers become 'Urjadata' (energy providers).
Implementation: Managed by the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE).
Central financial support of ₹34,422 crore (approx.) for targets up to March 2026.





4} PM-KMY (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maan-Dhan Yojana)


Objective: Social security and old-age pension for SMFs.
benefits: Minimum assured pension of ₹3,000/month after age 60; 50% family pension (₹1,500) for spouse.
Eligibility: SMFs (up to 2 hectares land), aged 18-40 years.
Contribution: Voluntary & contributory; farmer & govt match contributions (₹55-₹200/month).
Fund Manager: Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC).
